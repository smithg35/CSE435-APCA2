Prototype 1

For our prototype we decided to have buttons that would automatically compute the 10 given scenarios. We also thought it would be a good idea to have a form at the bottom that could be used to compute custom scenarios. We do not have our algorithm implemented yet, but once we do, the results of each scenario will be presented in the gray box at the bottom of the page. The results will display if the pedestrian is hit by the car or not, it will also show how much time is lost by slowing down the car when avoiding a pedestrian.
