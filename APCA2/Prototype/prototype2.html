APCA System Simulator Prototype

by GReEN Team




To simulate the APCA System, click the "Compute" button next to one of the scenarios in the tables beolow, and then click the "Run" button near the bottom of the page. If you want to activate the fail safe mode, make sure you have the checkbox checked before you click "Run". You must reset the simulator before running a new scenario.

Once the user has clicked the "Run" button, the scenario will be run through the simulator. A timer in the upper-right corner will keep track of how much time has past since the simulation began. If the pedestrian makes the vehicle come to a complete stop, a message will be displayed to show it is a "throw away scenario". If the scenario is successful, the time lost will be displayed.

The vehicle with be depicted by this orange rectangle.

The pedestrian will be depicted by this blue circle.

Vehicle's Initial Speed : 50 (kph)
Vehicle Width : 2 (m)
Pedestrian Initial Position : 35 (m)
